PROJECTS

TrikeGo – Local Tricycle Booking System

A web-based and mobile-friendly platform designed to modernize local transportation. Instead of waiting on the roadside, students and residents can conveniently book a tricycle ride from their phones. The system allows users to request rides, track availability, and connect directly with nearby drivers. It promotes safety, efficiency, and accessibility while giving local tricycle drivers a digital way to reach more passengers.

A local online marketplace for fresh fish and seafood, connecting multiple local vendors directly to customers. A web-based system designed for the CSU Library computer lab. Previously, students had to log their usage manually a process prone to unclear handwriting, manipulation, and inefficiency.
